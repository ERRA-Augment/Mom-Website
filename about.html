About


Christine offers upholstery sewing and embroidery services for large restaraunts, coffee shops, home furniture, antiques, marine upholstery and custom made cushions for windows ,benches, recreational vehicles.

Christine Halovich began her sewing career at the age of 9. A hobby for most of her young life developed into a labor of love . Under the guidance of her father Tom Gauthier a world famous artist of snooker cues. His attention to detail and expecting no less than perfection from his work with his utmost respect for his customers was instilled into her life as the true foundation of success. Christine went on to training in customer service and developing her skills as a seamstress in a local upholstery shop. She spent many years collecting equipment and machines needed to Have a fully equipped upholstery studio. As well has developed working relationships with various upholsterers, wood workers and suppliers . She believes in order to have a satisfied customer requires many different doors to be opened to ensure they have exactly what they need. Christine has created a lovely well equipped studio attached to her home nestled in beautiful Maple ridge. Christine believes that eliminating the high cost of a city store front enables her to extend fair prices to her customers without compromising on the workmanship she offers to her customers.
